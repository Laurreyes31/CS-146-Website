Dr. Janet K. Kennedy

Dr. Kennedy has been helping adults and teenagers form better sleeping habits for almost 20 years. She also helps patients with other underlying issues such as depression, anxiety, and life transitions. Dr. Kennedy provides cognitive-behavioral therapy, psychodynamic therapy, and cognitive behavioral therapy. She believes in improving mental health and sleep habits with her practices. You can book an appointment with her in her New York City office or join a virtual appointment.

Dr. Courtney F. Bancroft

Dr. Bancroft has Yale Training and creates specialized plans for each patient to help treat their insomnia. She has experience with treating insomnia, anxiety, and grief for over a decade, and she is expanding her practice in response to COVID-19. Dr. Bancroft specializes in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia and personalized treatments. Her website includes a free 15 minute consultation call, one-on-one therapy sessions tailored towards the patient, and telehealth appointments online.

Dr. Bancroft

NYU Langone

A team of professionals associated with NYU Langone to diagnose and treat sleeping disorders. There are seven doctors located in offices across Manhattan who usually take in-person office appointments. There is an emergency, virtual care video call if needed. They have a large team all over the United States with a variety of different treatments for any problem you may have. Every doctor has their own personal page as well for any research needed before booking an appointment.

Singular Sleep

Founded by Dr. Joseph Krainin, Singular Sleep helps you schedule an appoointment with a Sleep Doctor. They remotely diagnose and treat sleep apnea to provide equal access to everyone. Singular Sleep has several resources to help anyone whether they are seeking consultation or looking to buy equipment to improve their sleep apnea. Their shop page has discounted CPAP, BiPAP, and masks available to buy which could be very useful if it is needed.
